1. Information We Collect (Local Processing)

CADwari 2D Converter is designed to process DWG and DXF files selected by the user for conversion and viewing purposes. All file conversion is performed locally on your device.

  • Files selected by the user for conversion or viewing.
  • Basic app usage information required for normal offline functioning.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or files on external servers.

2. How We Use Information

The information accessed by the App is used strictly to provide core features such as file selection, conversion, storage, and viewing of converted content directly on your Android device.

3. File Access and Storage Permissions

The App requires permission to access files or media on your device so that selected CAD files can be read, converted, and saved. These files are processed only for the functionality requested by the user.

We do not sell, rent, upload, or intentionally share your files or technical drawings with any third parties.
